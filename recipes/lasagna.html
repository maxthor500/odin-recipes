Lasagna

Description:

The Lasagna is an institution. This rich and tasty dish originates from the Emilia region. Lasagna is known, appreciated, made, remade, tasted and shared everywhere. Layers of green egg pasta, dressed with the classic traditional ragu, bechamel and grated cheese. You just need to prepare it and enjoy it with your family for a very special day!

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Beef
  • Pancetta bacon
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Yellow onoions
  • Red wine
  • Tomato puree
  • Vegetable broth
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fne salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter
  • Whole milk
  • Flour
  • Nutmeg
  • Parmigiano cheese

Preparation:

  • Meat sauce:

    1. Prepare the vegetable broth and ensure to keep it warm.
    2. Cut the pancetta bacon into strips amd chop it well.
    3. Finely chop the carrots, onions, celery and set aside.
    4. In a saucepan, pour a drizzle of oil and the pancetta bacon.
    5. When the pancetta is brown, add the chopped vegetables and cook 5-6 minutes.
    6. Add the minced meat, stir and raise the heat.
    7. Deglaze with red wine, after the meat is brown slowly, and let evaporate.
    8. Add the tomato puree and cook for two hours.
    9. When it starts boiling again, add some hot broth and let cook for another couple of hours.

  • Bechamel sauce:

    1. Heat the milk in a saucepan, without boiling it.
    2. In another pan, place the butter and let it melt.
    3. Add the flour in one go and cook for a few minutes until you get a golden roux.
    4. Pour the hot milk in three times, mixing well.
    5. Add salt and flavor with nutmeg. Continue to mix until you get a creamy béchamel.

  • Composition Lasagna:

    1. Take a large 12x8 inches (30x20 cm) lasagna pan and grease the bottom.
    2. Add a thin layer of bechamel and one of Bolognese sauce.
    3. Place the first sheet.
    4. Add a layer of bechamel and a layer of meat sauce.
    5. Add grated Parmesan cheese.
    6. place another sheet of pasta on top.
    7. Continue like this until you have 5 layers.
    8. After placing the last sheet, add the Bolognese sauce.
    9. Sprinkle with grated cheese.
    10. Bake in a preheated static oven at 338°F (170°C) for 40 minutes.
