Lasagna

Description:

The Lasagna is an institution. This rich and tasty dish originates from the Emilia region. Lasagna is known, appreciated, made, remade, tasted and shared everywhere. Layers of green egg pasta, dressed with the classic traditional ragù, béchamel and grated cheese. You just need to prepare it and enjoy it with your family for a very special day!
