Tiramisu

Description:

This is the best Italian dessert.

Ingredients:

  • Ladyfingers
  • Eggs
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Sugar
  • Coffee
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder

Preparation:

  1. Separate the egg whites from the yolks.
  2. Whip the egg yolks with a hand mixer, pouring in only half of the sugar.
  3. Add mascarpone cheese, little by little.
  4. When all the cheese is incorporated, you will have obtained a cream.
  5. Beat the egg whites, pouring in the remaining sugar a little at a time.
  6. Whisk them into stiff peaks.
  7. Take a spoonful of egg whites and pour it into the bowl with egg yolks and sugar and stir vigorously with a spatula.
  8. Then proceed to add the remaining egg whites, little by little, stirring very gently from bottom to top.
  9. Place a generous spoonful of cream on the bottom of a 12x8in baking pan and spread it evenly.
  10. Then soak the ladyfingers for a few moments in the cooled coffee.
  11. Arrange the soaked ladyfingers on top of the cream.
  12. Create the first layer, over which you will spread part of the mascarpone cheese cream.
  13. Continue to arrange the coffee-soaked ladyfingers, then add another layer of cream
  14. Sprinkle it with unsweetened cocoa powder on the surface.
  15. Set in the fridge for a couple of hours.
